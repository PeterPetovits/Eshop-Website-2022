Analysis

With Apple's M1 chip designed for professionals, you get breakthrough performance and long battery life.
Add to that a great Liquid Retina XDR display, the best camera and sound you've ever had on a Mac laptop,
and all the ports you need.Users who have bought it stand out mainly because it has a good screen and the battery

Incredibly fast

The M1 Pro takes the outstanding performance of the M1 architecture
to a whole new level for professional users. Even the most
ambitious projects are easily handled with up to 10 CPU cores,
up to 16 GPU cores, 16 Neural Engine cores, and dedicated
media engine encoders and decoders that support H.264, HEVC, and ProRes codecs.

Unsurpassed screen

Liquid Retina XDR is the best display a laptop has ever had.
It has Extreme Dynamic Range and a million-to-one contrast ratio.
HDR content comes to life in photos, videos, and games with incredible,
shadow detail and vivid true-to-life colors. Each monitor is factory
calibrated and features professional reference functions
for HDR color grading, photography, design and print production.

Perfect camera performance

Staying in touch is more important than ever.
That's why the new MacBook Pro camera has double the resolution (1080p)
and uses a lens with a larger aperture that lets in more light.
Along with a larger image sensor that has more efficient pixels,
the camera offers twice the performance in low-light conditions.

3D sound

Thanks to its six-speaker system and advanced algorithms,
MacBook Pro supports room-filling sound when playing music
or videos with Dolby Atmos, creating a sophisticated
three-dimensional soundstage. Combine that with the Liquid Retina
XDR display and it's like having a home cinema.

Greater connectivity than ever before

Transfer photos and videos with an SDXC card reader.
Connect to TVs or monitors with HDMI output.
Listen using a 3.5mm headphone jack that automatically detects
and adjusts for high-impedance headphones. Connect to high-speed
peripherals or displays with three Thunderbolt 4 ports.
Fast charging using the MagSafe 3 quick-release port.
